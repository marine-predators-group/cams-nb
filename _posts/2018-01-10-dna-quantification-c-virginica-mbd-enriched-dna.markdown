Results:

Quantification Spreadsheet (Google Sheet): [20180110_qubit_dsDNA_BR_MBD_virginica](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1HVwlkcCn2AmhJQAHbP4GYSD-V4-ZnahiD55kc9PdjYs/edit?usp=sharing)
